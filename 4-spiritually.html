Link between religion and happiness

The Pew Research Center (United States) study “Religion’s Relationship to Happiness, Civic Engagement and Health Around the World” hits the link between happiness and religion, among other things. The findings of this study have been summarized in their article “Are religious people happier, healthier?”. The study involved people from the United-States and 25 other countries among South Africa, the Netherlands and Japan.
One of the findings is that participation in a religious community clearly is linked with higher levels of happiness and civic engagement (PRC 2019,p.6).

Reference: The Pew Research Center. Religion’s Relationship to Happiness, Civic Engagement and Health Around the World. 2019- (PRC 2019)
